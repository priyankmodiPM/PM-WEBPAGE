Enter My World






I’m a student developer-designer studying currently at IIIT Hyderabad. I’m fascinated by the intersection of Design,Mathematics and Technology. I look forward to working on projects which harness users cognitive and affective side. It’s a thing for me that people feel and tangibly react to the screen experience.

Graphic designing and puzzle solving have been things I’ve always enjoyed working on. They are much more than a passtime for me. Leading through the front and working with teams by acknowledging everyone's contribution and piling on their strengths is something I’m really good at. 

To know more about me check the About Me page. Feel free to contact me for any suggestions or to order sketches for free.
