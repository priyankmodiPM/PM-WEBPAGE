My Story

Born and brought up all around North India, Chandigarh has been my hometown since 11 years now. I passed out class 10 from St.Anne's Convent School, Chandigarh. Sports were always something that kindled a new fire to perform and enjoy. I played Softball at the state at UT level for 4 years. Sketching, doodling was my favourite way to pass the boring lectures. No, it wasn't all boring at sschool, I got a chance to explore the world of designing, coding and web development. It was during this time that I made a live-project And then, like most other Indians, JEE happened. No TV, no sports(though I did try to get involved in them for some time), just JEE. But, as they say, nothing is too bad to not have it's good moments. I explored the amazing world of Mathematics, credits to my institute professor. I scored a 100 at a test, something that happened after 15 years. It didn't stop there, I went on to learn a lot of new stuff, the applications of mathematics in origami and design perscpectives, the use of calculus and game theory and much more. JEE didn't really go how I wanted it to, but I got into my dream institute in a field of my interest. I joined the computational Linguistics programme in IIIT Hyderabad (not IIT, mind the 3rd I). I made a lot of friends and also became a member of the Students Parliament here, carrying on streak of making it to the house since school. And the journey continues, hoping to reach the skies.....

Interests

Have you ever tried counting the number of stars in the sky. Don't worry, neither have I. What I have done is trying to search for all possible shades of different colors, my last count being 632. I won't call myself an artist, doodler sounds cooler. Most of my comic characters and other stuff is done on the margins of my notebooks, either during class or anytime after.

I really enjoy working on graphics and designing which is the reason I made most of the official movies at school. Puzzle solving has been my thing always, I still cant forget the number of hours I have wasted in solving problems even when I had a test the next day. I am a die hard cricket fan, not having missed a single match since the 2007 world cup. I really love playing cricket, softball and baseball. Yes folks, baseball, it's not an easy sport. Mathematics and MAT problems is something I enjoy a lot, Cheryl's Birthday, the 25 horses problem etc. are some of my favourites. I really love wathching movie, Shawshank Redemption, The Pursuit of Happiness and The Green Mile among my Top 3

Working with teams and leading them is my thing and I good at that. I made it to the school parliament twice, and again went on to be a Member of Parliament at IIIT. I feel I connect well with people and everyone finds me really approachable. There's still a lot of things to achieve, a lot of passions to fulfil. But no matter which field i work in, I just want to enjoy working with my team and bringing out more than what I actually think of myself.
